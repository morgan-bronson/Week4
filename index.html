Steve Jobs

Steve Jobs contribution to the computer world was founding Apple, Pixar and NeXT. Apple is the biggest company that sells phones, ipads, macbooks, etc. Steve Jobs and his co-founders are mainly known for being the masterminds behind all the apple devices and for making history when they created the first iphone. They are the reasons we can text and communicate through devices faster. Something Jobs did that many people don't know is that he saved Apple from going broke. When Apple was in a financial problem Jobs made the decision to cut the company's research lab.



Jobs was born on February 24, 1955 in San Fransisco, California and was given up for adoption. He was adopted into a family where he met his parents Clara and Paul Jobs and his sister Patricia Ann Jobs. Later in his life he met his biological sister Mona Simpson. In 1978 Jobs had his first child Lisa Brennan-Jobs. Then in 1991 he married his wife Lawrene Paul and had his next 3 children Reed, Erin Sienna and Eve. Jobs sadly died of Neuroendocrine Cancer on October 5th, 2011 in Palo Alto, California.
